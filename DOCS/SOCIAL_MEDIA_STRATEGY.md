## 1. Tone & Voice Guidelines

### ‚ùå What to AVOID (The "AI Trap")
- **Over-hyping**: "Unleash the power", "Ultimate", "Game-changer", "Masterpiece".
- **Robotic Transitions**: "In conclusion", "Dive into", "Here is the breakdown".
- **Aggressive/Hard Masculine**: "Dominate", "Destroy", "Brutal".
- **Fake Emotion**: "I am thrilled to announce", "So excited to share".

### ‚úÖ The Target Voice ("Soft & Authentic")
- **Perspective**: First-person ("I", "My").
- **Emotion**: Honest, grounded, sharing the *process* not just the *result*.
- **Style**:
    - **Soft**: Use words like "Crafting", "Layering", "Feeling", "Mood".
    - **Feminine nuance**: Focus on aesthetics, color play, and the *feeling* of the piece, rather than just stats/lore.
    - **Conversational**: Like showing a friend a photo on your phone.
